Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2024

Your privacy is very important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when using our app.

1. Information We Collect

• Profile data (age, gender, height, weight, goals)
• Food records and meal photos
• App settings and preferences
• Usage data of app features

2. Use of Information

• Provide personalized nutrition recommendations
• Analyze food products using AI
• Track progress and achievements
• Improve app functionality
• Create backups of your data

3. Data Storage

• Locally on your device
• Encrypted in Google Drive (when backup is enabled)
• On AI service servers only during request processing
• We do not permanently store your personal data on our servers

4. Information Sharing

• We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties, except:
  - AI services for food analysis (data transmitted anonymously)
  - Google Drive for backup (with your consent)
  - Cases required by law

5. Data Security

• Data encryption during transmission and storage
• Regular security updates
• Limited data access
• Secure authentication methods

6. Your Rights

• Access your data
• Correct inaccurate information
• Delete your data
• Restrict data processing
• Transfer data to another service
• Withdraw consent for processing

7. Cookies and Analytics

• Local data to save settings
• Anonymous analytics to improve the app
• We do not use advertising trackers
• You can disable analytics in settings

8. Policy Changes

    We may update this privacy policy. We will notify you of significant changes through:
• In-app notifications
• App store updates
• Last update date is indicated at the beginning of the document

9. Contact Information

    If you have questions about our privacy policy, contact us:    
• Email: support@nutriai.app
• Feedback form in the app
• We will respond within 48 hours.
