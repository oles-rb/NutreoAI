NutreoAI – Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how NutreoAI ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, stores, and protects your information. By using our services, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Who We Are

NutreoAI is a mobile nutrition and wellness app that uses artificial intelligence to help users track food, analyze meals, and improve eating habits.

Data Controller:
NutreoAI (Individual Developer or Company Name)
Email: support@NutreoAI.app

2. Information We Collect

  • Profile data (age, gender, height, weight, goals)
  • Meal photos and food logs
  • App settings and preferences
  • Feature usage (e.g., number of food scans)
  • Crash reports and performance data (anonymous)

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Provide personalized nutrition insights
  • Analyze food using AI services
  • Track progress and recommend goals
  • Improve app performance and usability
  • Enable backups (with your permission)

We do not use your data for marketing or targeted advertising.

4. Where and How Your Data Is Stored

  • Locally on your device
  • Encrypted in Google Drive (if you enable backup)
  • Temporarily processed by AI servers (e.g., image recognition)

We do not store your personal data permanently on our own servers.

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell or trade your personal data. We only share limited data with:

  • AI services for analysis (data is anonymized)
  • Google Drive (only if you enable backup)
  • Legal authorities when required by law

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and review your data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your data
  • Restrict or object to data processing
  • Transfer your data to another service
  • Withdraw consent at any time (via settings or contact)

7. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13 (or 16 in some jurisdictions). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Cookies and Analytics

  • Local storage to save settings
  • Anonymous usage data to improve the app
  • No advertising or tracking cookies

You may disable analytics in app settings.

9. Security Measures

  • Encryption of data at rest and in transit
  • Secure authentication and storage practices
  • Limited access to your data
  • Regular security updates

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Major changes will be communicated via:

  • In-app messages
  • App store update notes

The last update date is shown at the top of this page.

11. Contact Us
